Song Guesser





How to play:

Sit down with a friend and play!
To begin, input a spotify playlist that the game will take songs from.
One person, the rater, will rate three songs from the playlist.
The other person, the guesser, will listen along and get to see the ratings for each song.
For the fourth song, the rater's rating will be hidden, and the guesser will have to use information from the prior songs to guess the rater's rating.
